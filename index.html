TBRFranklin Recovery Programs

Chronic Pain Management

Specialized programs for individuals dealing with chronic pain, combining manual therapy, movement exercises, and pain management techniques.

Holistic approaches to address the underlying causes of chronic pain and improve quality of life.

Functional Movement Assessments

In-depth assessments to identify movement patterns, imbalances, and weaknesses, leading to personalized corrective exercises.

Movement screenings for all age groups to prevent injuries and enhance overall movement efficiency.

Preventive Wellness Workshops

Educational workshops focusing on preventive measures, nutrition, and lifestyle choices for maintaining optimal health and well-being.

Wellness seminars cover the topics such as stress management, sleep hygiene, and mental health.

Mind-Body Integration

Integration of mindfulness practices, such as yoga and meditation, into rehabilitation and wellness programs.

Stress reduction techniques to complement physical therapies and enhance overall recovery.

Golf Performance

TBR Franklin’s Golf Performance programming is centered on you the Golfer to improve your strength, increase your mobility, and physically allow you to return to the Golf Course day after day at your very best.

Rehabilitation for Various Conditions

Specialized rehabilitation programs for individuals recovering from surgeries or injuries, tailored to their specific needs.

Post-surgery recovery plans which focus on regaining strength, mobility, and functionality.

Geriatric Physical Therapy

Comprehensive physical therapy programs for older adults addressing age-related mobility issues, balance, and pain management.

Fall prevention programs to enhance the safety and independence of older individuals.
